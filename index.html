About

Valley Clock Works is a single man clock repair shop founded in 1996, and was moved to its current location in September 1998. VCW is located in downtown Tell City on Main street.

Valley Clock Works is a factory authorized service center for Howard Miller Clocks and an authorized dealer for Kit Cat clocks.
